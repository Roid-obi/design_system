Adds a border to a given side of an element.

Valid sides are (`top`, `right`, `bottom`, `left`, `all`, `none`)

# Border Radius

Rounds the corners of the given side of an element.

Valid sides are (`top`, `right`, `bottom`, `left`, `all`, `none`)

Valid amounts are (`s`, `m`, `l`)

<Story name="Border Radius">
  <ClassExample
    signature="rounded--<side>(--<amount>)"
    classCategory={rounded}
    hideBorder
  >
    <div className="padding--all--xs bgColor--cloudGrey">
      Element with a border helper applied
    </div>
  </ClassExample>
</Story>
